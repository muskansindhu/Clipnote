Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Clipnote

Is Clipnote free to use?

Yes! Clipnote is completely free and open-source. You can use all features without any cost.

Which browsers are supported?

Clipnote works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Edge, and Brave.

How does AI note-taking work?

Our AI analyzes video transcripts and identifies key concepts, definitions, and important moments automatically.

Can I export my notes?

Yes, you can export your notes in multiple formats and share them with others easily.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Your notes are stored securely and you have full control over your data.
