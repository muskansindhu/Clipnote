Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Clipnote

Is Clipnote free to use?

Yes! Clipnote is completely free and open-source. You can use all features without any cost.

Which browsers are supported?

Clipnote currently supports Google Chrome. Support for other browsers is coming soon.

How does AI note-taking work?

Clipnote lets you take timestamped notes and uses the related transcript to generate precise AI notes synced to the video.

Can I export my notes?

We’re currently working on note export. You’ll be able to download and share your notes soon.
